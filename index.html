About

Safety that scales with the project.

I am a results-driven EHS/HSE professional who turns regulatory requirements into proactive safety cultures on the ground. My experience spans the full EPC spectrum — EHV power transmission and substations, semiconductor fab construction, EV gigafactories and utility-scale solar — where I lead HIRA/HAZOP risk assessments, IMS/ISO 45001 & 14001 audits, incident investigations, permit-to-work systems, and EHS training. Backed by an M.Tech in Industrial Safety Engineering from NIT Tiruchirappalli and certifications including NEBOSH HSE PSM, IOSH Managing Safely and ISO 45001, I pair field discipline with data — using Power BI and EHS digitization to make safety measurable.
